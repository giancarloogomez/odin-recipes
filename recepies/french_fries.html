Fetuccini Alfredo

A delicious plate of French Fries

The preferred side with any burger or Chicken Wings

Freshly Cut and Fried potatoes from Iowa

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Peanut Oil
  • Salt

Recipe Instructions

  1. Peel and Cut potatoes
  2. Bring oil to cooking temperature while peeling and cutting potatoes
  3. Add potatoes and deep fry them for a few minutes
  4. For best flavor deep fry fries a second time
  5. Finally add salt and enjoy
